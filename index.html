Hobbs

Luke Hobbs, also known as Agent Hobbs or simply known as Hobbs, is a highly skilled bounty hunter for the DSS and the lead federal agent whose job is to capture Dominic Toretto and his team in Fast Five, the quaternary main protagonist in Fast & Furious Franchise, and one of the main protagonists of the spinoff Hobbs and Shaw.
